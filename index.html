About


From a young age, I have been inspired to create; I dabbled in plenty of art forms: music, photography, and videography etc. I became intrigued with website design and development once I entered college. There I mastered the front-end programming languages: HTML, CSS, Javascript, and JQuery, but also became equipped to use the programs on the Adobe Creative Suite, so I could also comprehend the production of content.

Now that I graduated from Rochester Institute of Technology with a Bachelors of Science in Applied Arts & Science with a minor in Chinese, I am currently looking for a full-time position where I can flourish and be a part of a creative team, making an impact on the world.

Multilingual Website


For my capstone project, I decided to focus on RIT’s online publications; I realized they were only produced in English. What about the other popular languages that are prominent on the Internet? I created a mock website through Drupal 8 for one of the school’s news publications with the function to translate entire articles from English to Simplified Chinese, which would then be available to the reader.
